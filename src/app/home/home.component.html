Director's Message

Subramaniam V.
Director - Reliance Retail
With innovation as our fuel,We shall reach the pinnacle of success.The journey awaits, let's embark.

This includes those of us helming businesses and/or part of business teams, and the creative writers and "contemplatives", "ideators", whose works, thoughts, ideas propel us forward. We are each working towards improving organization's social and psychological life, along with, and in fact IN SYNC with, its material existence, its value-creating intrinsic trait that we power.

What is value creation? Value creation is the idea that VALUE IS NOT DISCOVERED, it is not in hiding somewhere, it is CREATED. Why so? This is because with time, needs, wants, desires change, evolve. In customer facing businesses, we discern needs and we build to solve for them. We are each creating Value in that sense. However, a stagnant, static repeat of what was always done, almost never leads to any benefit let along creation of value.
